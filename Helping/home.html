Come Online With Us!

This App will enable you to put your individual restaurant according to your customized menu on the internet in this phase of pandemic and for the future.
This application ensures online interaction between restaurant and customer will become more easy and small restaurant can have their foothold in this online arena. We have create an exchange environment so that nobody has to face loss at the end this app will help restaurant have their own website with customised dining takeaway and delivery options that you will be able to establish yourself on online front and can overcome these pandemic losses.


It is a simple adding menu items, tables, etc. in this app you will just have to write menu-items and click add. The customers can easily access the menus and order.
